One Handed

One handed bowling is the most common style to play bowling.

It is also the most traditional style as professional bowling started with only one handed bowlers.

The style consist of the bowler using their dominant hand to hold their ball using their thumb, middle and ring finger and their other non-dominant hand to use as the balance hand.

Professional bowlers who throw one handed are : Chris Barnes, EJ Tackett, Tommy Jones and more.

Two Handed

Two handed bowling style is the upcoming norm in bowling.

This style was first seen by Jason Belmonte and Osku Palermaa and has proven to have advantages over the usual one handed bowling style.

By using 2 hands, the bowler can keep their dominant hand underneath the ball and hence can generate more revolutions than typical one handed bowlers.

Professional bowlers who throw one handed are : Jason Belmonte, Jesper Svensson, Chris Via and more.
